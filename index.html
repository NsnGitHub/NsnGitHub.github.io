Hi, I'm Anson.

An aspiring software engineer currently enrolled in BCIT's Computer Systems Technology program.

Before all of this, I was a business student at SFU concentrating in either Finance or Accounting. I enjoyed working with numbers and data in order to gain insights and solve problems, but I didn't enjoy the other parts of it, so I thought about what I truly wanted to study.

About a year ago, I stumbled upon competitive programming videos on YouTube and I loved seeing how people used code to solve complex problems. It was at this point where I taught myself C++ and made my way through beginner problems on Codeforces. After about two months, I realized that I was passionate about create code to solve all of these problems, and that I enjoy doing so for a living. So I decided to pause my education at SFU to pursue a career in software development.
