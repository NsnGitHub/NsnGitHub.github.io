Hi, I'm Anson.

An aspiring software engineer currently enrolled in BCIT's Computer Systems Technology program.

Before all of this, I was a business student at SFU concentrating in either finance or accounting. I enjoyed working with numbers and data in order to gain insights and solve problems, but didn't enjoy the other parts as much. I researched other routes that I could take regarding my education, but nothing at that time piqued my interest more than finance.

However, about a year ago, I stumbled upon competitive programming videos on YouTube and loved seeing how people used code to solve complex problems. I decided to give it a try, so I taught myself C++ and made my way through beginner problems on Codeforces. After about two months, it occured to me that I was passionate about developing code to solve problems, and that I would enjoy doing it for a living. So, I decided to pause my education at SFU to pursue a career in software development.
